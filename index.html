About the Project

This ministry was founded on the belief that small acts of faith can yield great transformation. Just like the mustard seed, we aim to plant hope, love, and truth in communities through creative outreach and spiritual growth.

Community Outreach

We serve by building community gardens, hosting prayer walks, and creating spaces where people can connect, grow, and heal. Join us as we plant seeds of faith and action.
